I’ve got a home lab where I’m configuring a pseudo-enterprise environment, complete with virtualized bastion servers.

Network Equipment

Router: Ubiquiti EdgeRouter X

Crazy powerful box for $50. Nice WebUI, and has a hardware switch chip, so it does actual gigabit.

WAP: Asus RT-AC68U with Merlin Firmware

Great WAP, and it was a great router until I got gigabit Internet. I saved some money getting a T-Mobile-branded refurb on Amazon for $77, and reflashing it.

I get about 250-350Mbps on my two laptops with 2x2 antennas.

WAP: Cisco Aironet 1601e

I picked this up from a friend for cheap so I can get more Cisco in my life. Since wireless N maxes out around 150Mbps for 2x2 antennas, it’s been relegated to serving my guest WiFi.

I thought about selling it, but I put $40 into antennas, and these things sell for $50 on a good day. It’s going to serve as my guest WiFi.

Switch: Cisco Catalyst WS-C2960G-8TC-L

Not much to say about this guy. VLAN tagging along with the
